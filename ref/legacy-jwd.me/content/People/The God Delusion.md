The God Delusion is [[Richard Dawkins]]' popular-level argument against theistic belief and in favor of a naturalistic worldview.

The book became a major reference point in the "new atheist" era and kicked off a lot of public debate.

Even if people disagree with the tone, it clearly influenced the conversation.
