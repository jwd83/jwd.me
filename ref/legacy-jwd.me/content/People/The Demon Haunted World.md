The Demon Haunted World is [[Carl Sagan]]'s case for skepticism, scientific literacy, and intellectual self-defense against nonsense.

The "baloney detection kit" framing is probably the part people remember most.

If I had to hand somebody one book for epistemic hygiene, this is always in the shortlist.
