# Favorite Musicians
I keep coming back to these artists and composers.

## [[Christopher Tin]]
`Baba Yetu` was the track that first hooked me. `Dream of Flight` is another favorite and has the same big, uplifting feel.

## [[Borislav Slavov]]
The DOS2/BG3 music is excellent and does a lot of heavy lifting for the mood of those games.

## [[Evan Call]]
His music for Frieren is some of my favorite anime scoring period.

## [[Christopher Larkin]]
I love what he did for Hollow Knight, and I'm looking forward to what he does with Silksong.

## [[Pink Floyd]]
One of my all-time favorite bands. [[Time]] is still my favorite track.

## [[Daft Punk]]
Countless songs I like and revisit constantly.

## [[String Cheese Incident]]
An old high school friend's uncle is in the band. My favorite track is [[Good Times Around the Bend]].

## [[John Williams]]
The Star Wars themes are some of the most recognizable music ever written.

## [[Howard Shore]]
His Lord of the Rings scores are all-timers.

## [[Nobuo Uematsu]]
So much of my RPG nostalgia is tied to his [[Final Fantasy]] and Square-era work.
