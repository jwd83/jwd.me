## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Sony Computer Entertainment
- First release: November 11, 2006 (Japan); November 17, 2006 (US)
- Designed by: Sony hardware team (Cell architecture with IBM and Toshiba)
- CPU: Cell Broadband Engine (3.2 GHz)
- Memory: 256 MB XDR RAM + 256 MB GDDR3 VRAM
- Graphics: Nvidia RSX "Reality Synthesizer"
- Game format: Blu-ray Disc, DVD, CD
