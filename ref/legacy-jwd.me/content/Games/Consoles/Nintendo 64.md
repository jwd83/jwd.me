## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Nintendo
- First release: June 23, 1996 (Japan); September 26, 1996 (US)
- Designed by: Nintendo (project led by Genyo Takeda), with SGI partnership
- CPU: NEC VR4300 (93.75 MHz)
- Memory: 4 MB Rambus RDRAM (8 MB with Expansion Pak)
- Graphics: SGI Reality Coprocessor (RCP)
- Game format: Cartridges
