## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Nintendo
- First release: February 14, 2003 (Japan); March 23, 2003 (US)
- Designed by: Nintendo handheld hardware team
- CPU: ARM7TDMI (16.78 MHz)
- Memory: 256 KB external WRAM + 32 KB internal WRAM + 96 KB VRAM
- Display: 240x160 LCD (frontlit/backlit depending revision)
- Game format: Cartridges
- Battery: Rechargeable lithium-ion
