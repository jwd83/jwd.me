## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Nintendo
- First release: July 15, 1983 (Japan, as Famicom); October 18, 1985 (US, as NES)
- Designed by: Nintendo R&D2 (led by Masayuki Uemura)
- CPU: Ricoh 2A03/2A07 (6502-based, ~1.79 MHz NTSC)
- Memory: 2 KB RAM, 2 KB VRAM (base)
- Graphics: Ricoh 2C02 PPU
- Game format: Cartridges
