## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Nintendo
- First release: March 3, 2017 (worldwide)
- Designed by: Nintendo Platform Technology Development group
- CPU/GPU: Nvidia Tegra X1-based SoC
- Memory: 4 GB LPDDR4
- Storage: 32 GB internal (base model), microSD expansion
- Display: 6.2-inch 1280x720 LCD (base handheld)
- Game format: Game cards and digital downloads
