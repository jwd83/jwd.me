## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Sony Computer Entertainment
- First release: March 4, 2000 (Japan); October 26, 2000 (US)
- Designed by: Sony team led by Ken Kutaragi
- CPU: Emotion Engine (294.9 MHz)
- Memory: 32 MB RDRAM
- Graphics: Graphics Synthesizer (147 MHz)
- Game format: DVD-ROM and CD-ROM
