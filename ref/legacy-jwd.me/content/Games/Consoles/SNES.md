## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Nintendo
- First release: November 21, 1990 (Japan); August 23, 1991 (US)
- Designed by: Nintendo R&D2 (led by Masayuki Uemura)
- CPU: Ricoh 5A22 (~3.58 MHz, variable)
- Memory: 128 KB RAM, 64 KB VRAM
- Graphics: Nintendo PPU chipset (Mode 7 support)
- Game format: Cartridges
