## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Sony Computer Entertainment
- First release: December 3, 1994 (Japan); September 9, 1995 (US)
- Designed by: Sony team led by Ken Kutaragi
- CPU: MIPS R3000A (33.87 MHz)
- Memory: 2 MB RAM, 1 MB VRAM
- Graphics: Sony custom PS1 GPU
- Game format: CD-ROM
