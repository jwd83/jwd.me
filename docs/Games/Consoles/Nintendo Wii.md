## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Nintendo
- First release: November 19, 2006 (US)
- Designed by: Nintendo IRD (hardware group led by Genyo Takeda)
- CPU: IBM PowerPC "Broadway" (729 MHz)
- Memory: 88 MB total (24 MB + 64 MB)
- Graphics: ATI "Hollywood"
- Game format: Wii optical discs (plus GameCube discs)
