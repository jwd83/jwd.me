## Quick Specs
- Manufacturer: Nintendo
- First release: November 1, 2008 (Japan); April 5, 2009 (US)
- Designed by: Nintendo handheld hardware team
- CPU: ARM9 + ARM7 (faster clock than original DS)
- Memory: 16 MB RAM
- Storage: 256 MB internal flash + SD card support
- Display: Dual 3.25-inch screens (256x192 each)
- Game format: Nintendo DS cartridges and DSiWare digital titles
