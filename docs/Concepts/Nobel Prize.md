The Nobel Prize is one of the most famous international awards for major contributions in science, literature, peace, and economics.

The original categories were Physics, Chemistry, Physiology or Medicine, Literature, and Peace; the economics prize was added later.

It is often used as a shorthand signal for world-class impact, even though awards are always a bit political and imperfect.
